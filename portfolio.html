About

Born and raised in Singapore, I was always brought up to follow The Standard Path™ which is to get a university degree and work. However at 19, I decided to enter the workforce as soon as possible so I could make my own money. I loved arts and expressing myself but there were not many stable jobs readily available in those industries. Because I couldn’t find many people similar to me, I started a Tik Tok account sharing my experiences to connect with people. Doing Tik Tok taught me a lot about how to use my voice to resonate with and attract the right people. I applied this knowledge and skillset to work as a social media content specialist.
